College Fund

Planning for college expenses is a significant financial goal, especially for single women who may be managing finances independently. Understanding the various options for college savings accounts can help you make informed decisions and secure a bright future for your children or yourself. Investing in a 529 account, custodial accounts, or other college savings plans can provide tax benefits and flexibility in how funds are used.
